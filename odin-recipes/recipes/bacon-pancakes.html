Creamy Mushroom Noodles with White Whine Sauce

Decription

Ingredients

  • pasta. Any shape works, long or short – I love farfalle, tagliatelle, mafalde, or spirali.
  • garlic
  • mushrooms (I used chestnut mushrooms, but just choose your favourites)
  • cream (single or double work fine)
  • vegetable stock
  • fresh parsley
  • dry white wine
  • onion

Steps

  1. Sauté the mushrooms and onion in butter.
  2. Add the white wine and simmer for a few minutes to cook off the alcohol.
  3. Add the cream, and bring together until you have a luscious, creamy white wine sauce.
  4. Serve with pasta and plenty of fresh parsley.
